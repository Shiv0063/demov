About Us

Indigoseaways Pvt. Ltd under the brand name of DG Sea Connect - A Detox Group Company is India’s first Roll On-Roll Off Ferry Service provider- the unique & fastest Passenger cum Cargo service in partnership with Gujarat Maritime Board in Gulf of Cambay. DG Sea Connect is one such dream seen by our Hon. Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi to bring India's first ROPAX Ferry into existence which connects two crucial points of Gujarat - Ghogha(Bhavnagar)& Hazira(Surat).

The project successfully accomplishes the goal of reducing the distance between Hazira(Surat and Ghogha(Bhavnagar) from 360km &10-12 hours (roadways) to 90km &4 hours (seaways) while giving cost effective travelling solutions to people. This project aims to take the amount of carbon footprint to a remarkable reduction by saving the exhaustion of fuel, and thus decongesting roads and lessening air pollution for environmental preservation. Our services will promote employment/business opportunities along with significant reduction in the travelling time, cost & preserving the environment.
